### 👤 About Me

I am a passionate **Cybersecurity student** pursuing my **M.Sc. in Digital Forensics & Information Technology** at the **National Forensic Sciences University (NFSU)**. 

My interest lies at the intersection of network security, defensive tool engineering, and forensic analysis. I enjoy reverse-engineering attacks, developing automation scripts for intrusion detection, and performing host and network forensics. I am active in building security tools to detect and mitigate malicious network activities (like ARP spoofing, DDoS, and Smurf attacks) in real-time.

* 🎓 **Academic Institution:** National Forensic Sciences University (NFSU)
* 🛡️ **Fields of Interest:** Incident Response, Network Forensics, Offensive Security, Secure Software Engineering
* 💬 **Ask me about:** Network traffic analysis, Wireshark, Scapy, intrusion detection mechanisms
* 🌐 **Connect with me:** [GitHub Profile](https://github.com/meet711)
